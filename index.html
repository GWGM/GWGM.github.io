The Dream

The beat-generation

Hvdwiink has been playing with sound design since about 2013 & finishing tracks since late 2015. He has garnished a small following(700 soundcloud followers & 100k+ plays) and is now looking forward to releasing his music under the his newely created label GWGM. Good Women Good Men Productions is a hub of mellow trippy trap & smooth trancy EDM. We look forward to getting more indie producers on-board to make a bang (& a buck) in the music industry.

Our Work Process.

Explore.

From spotify to soundcloud the way we experience music has changed. The power of the internet has allowed anyone with access to explore the endless bounds of human expression, through synthesized sound humanity pushes the bounds of sonic experience.

Design.

Using the latest VST's like: nexus, sylenth1, trillian, serum, etc. surreal sounds can be designed. We look for the weirdest & trippiest sounds that will create memorable experiences.

Develop.

The only true way to develop crisp sounds is to listen. Whether your using a free VST or the most expensive orchestra plugin it won't change the production as much as actually taking the time to listen & feel the track.

Deliver.

Deliver the most nasty sounds imaginable. Innovate new ways of creating lasting experiences. Move people with not only a solid production quality but also a memorable visual experience(learn that Photoshop son).

Software

Technology + Creative mind = Art!

FL Studio & Ableton Live

FL studio has made itself a staple in the trap industry, with it's ease of access and regular updates it is still a solid option for laying down beats. Ableton live is an excellent DAW for modern music production and has a lot of versatility as a program(remixing tracks/live performance). If you are submitting for a collaboration either of these formats would be ideal(or the stems)

Affinity Designer & Photoshop

For mockups/designing cover art these are the go-to programs unless your on linux(gimp is a decent option), if you enjoy the art you see and want something similar created for you either consider learning either of those programs or forward us your ideas we love all flavours of art not just the sound!
